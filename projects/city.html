City Projects

Ramaswami's team has worked with several cities on individual projects where we support cities in taking an inventory of their energy use and carbon emissions and strategizing pathways toward future sustainable infrastructure planning. We are working with cities in Colorado (Denver, Bloomfield, Arvada, and others), as well as cities and counties in the Midwestern US, including in Dane County, WI.

Check out the press release for the Denver plan

In Minnesota, our group has collaborated with partners including LHB, GPI and the Energy Transitions Lab to co-develop a tool that would help cities in identifying high impact and cost-effective pathways towards low carbon and sustainable infrastructures.
