Professional Societies and Advisory Boards

I enjoy interdisciplinary collaborations fostered at the International Society for Industrial Ecology, where I’ve served on the board and in particular, I've served on the Sustainable Urban Systems section of the ISIE.

The International Society for Industrial Ecology promotes industrial ecology as a way of finding innovative solutions to complicated environmental problems, and facilitates communication among scientists, engineers, policymakers, managers and advocates who are interested in better integrating environmental concerns with economic activities. Learn more about ISIE.

Professional Societies

I serve on the following societies:

  • Association for Environmental Engineering and Science Professors (AEESP)

    • AEESP assists its members in improving education and research programs, encourages graduate education, and serves the profession by providing information to government agencies and the public, and provides direct benefits to its members. Learn more about AEESP.

  • American Society for Civil Engineering (ASCE)

    • ASCE is a leading provider of technical and professional conferences and continuing education, the world’s largest publisher of civil engineering content, and an authoritative source for codes and standards that protect the public. Learn more here about ASCE.
